HOSPITAL FAQS

WHAT DOES iCLICK MED (ICM) DO?

iClick Med (ICM) is a ‘one-stop’ online platform which matches Locum Doctors to the NHS. iClick Med streamlines and expedites the entire locum recruitment process from registration through to time-sheets and payments. It is the smart way hospitals can quickly fill their staffing rota gaps with locum doctors who are available in real time.

 

WHAT INSPIRED YOU TO FORM ICM?

iClick Med was established in 2014 by two medical doctors who work in the NHS. Dr Manny Chawla and Dr Anne Najjuka witnessed and experienced the gaps in NHS financing and staff rotas that challenged patient safety and continuity of patient care. Over lunch in July 2014, our co-founders realised the urgent need for a 'one-stop' platform directly connecting Locum Doctors to the NHS.

 

IS THE WEBSITE SECURE?

Yes! iClick Med has been built with a very secure and versatile technology. We meet the vigorous legal requirements for storing your personal information. You are safe with us! 

 

HOW DOES IT WORK?

I WANT TO JOIN! HOW CAN I REGISTER?

1. Click on the ‘Register’ button and then enter your hospital's details, choose a secure password and submit. 

2. Check you email for the activation link and the unique username we send. Don’t forget to check your spam

    box!

3. Click on the activation link sent and it will lead you to your Dashboard.

4. Go to my ‘My Area’, select ‘My Settings’ to complete your registration. 

 

WHAT HAPPENS AFTER I REGISTER?

Once verified (normally within 1 working day), you can start to use ICM to post jobs and find locums.

 

TELL ME ABOUT 'MY DASHBOARD'?

ICM's dashboard helps you to work efficiently and stay organised. Your dashboard gives you a ‘bird’s eye view’ of your total expendiure, total jobs posted (colour coded calender to indicate if the job posted has been filled or unfilled).  A map of your locality to shows all doctors near you.

 

TELL ME ABOUT 'MY JOBS'?

ICM categorises all your posted jobs into: Total, Unfilled, Expired and Archived. See the details of each job category by using our easy to use jobs filter.

 

CAN I EDIT A POSTED JOB?

Yes. Go to Jobs and click on edit.

 

HOW DO I BOOK DOCTORS?

1.   Click on 'Jobs'.

2.   Click on 'Search Jobs' to find your posted job.

3.   Click on 'Details'.

4.   Click on 'Invite more locums'.

5.   Use the job filters on this page to find locums according to your requirements,

6.   Click on the Locum's name to view and download his/her's CV (please note: locum's contact details on the

      CV are censored until a locum has been booked)

7.   Click on 'Invite' to shortlist the locum

8.   If a locum accepts your initial invite, he/she will appear in the 'Shortlisted locums' tab

9.   Click on the 'Shortlisted locums' to view all the locums that have accepted your invite (Don't forget to click on

      the locum's name to remind you of his/her's CV).

10. Click on 'Offer' next the locum you would like to book. He/she will then appear on the 'Selected locums' tab

      where you can now view and download the locum's full CV with contact details.

 

WHAT IS A LOCUM BANK?

ICM gives you the opportunity to grow your own bank of external locum doctors. Bank locums are those who have been booked and completed a job at your Trust​ to your satisfaction. 

 

CAN I MANAGE MY STAFF BANK USING ICM?

ICM can indeed be used to manage your Trust’s Bank staff more efficiently. Simply drop us an email at hello@iclickmed.com for more information.

 

HOW DO I RECEIVE TIME-SHEETS? 

As soon as you book a locum doctor, a time-sheet reflecting the booked hours is auto-generated for the locum. At the end of the shift, the locum doctor updates the timesheet online and then prints it for authentication. The locum will then upload the signed copy on ICM for your approval.

  

HOW DO I KEEP TRACK OF MY TIME-SHEETS?

Each job will have a job number which also forms the timesheet and invoice number. ICM will auto-generate unique numbers each assignment. All uploaded scanned copies of time-sheets can be downloaded for storage as soft or hard copies. Copies of uploaded time-sheets are only kept on ICM for three months.

 

HOW DO I PAY THE LOCUM DOCTOR?

Depending on the employment status of the locum doctor, an invoice with the appropriate details will be auto-generated by ICM.
