About us

Core Objectives:

1. Be the cheapest, quickest and most efficient recruitment model for matching Locum Doctors to NHS vacancies.

2. Be bastions of transparency, use cutting edge technology and digitalisation to help tackle the spiralling temporary staffing costs in our NHS. 

3. Put patients at the heart of every decision we make.

4. Be a neutral ecosystem that works for all agency categories and bank models whilst remaining a central hub for all locum related activities.

Who we are:

 

Over lunch in July 2014, Dr Manny Chawla and Dr Anne Najjuka shared their frustration at the inefficiency of the locum recruitment process and the need for a 'one-stop' online platform directly connecting Locum Doctors to the NHS. They put these words into action and called this platform iClick Med.

 

Our founders have worked extremely hard with our developers to deliver iClick Med's core objectives. They have listened to Junior Doctors, Consultants, Hospitals and Agencies to make iClick Med user-friendly and tailoring it to your wants and needs. Productivity and engagement from people with all levels of computer skills and equipment is assured. Sign up today for free in a just a few clicks!
