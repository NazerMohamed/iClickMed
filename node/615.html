Locum FAQs

 

 WHAT DOES iCLICK MED (ICM) DO?

iClick Med (ICM) is a ‘one-stop’ online platform which matches locum doctors to the NHS. The solution streamlines and expedites the locum recruitment process from requisition through to remuneration. It is the smart way hospitals can quickly fill their staffing rota gaps with locum doctors who are available in real time.

 

WHAT INSPIRED YOU TO FORM ICM?

iClick Med was established in 2014 by two medical doctors who work in the NHS. Dr Manny Chawla and Dr Anne Najjuka witnessed and experienced the gaps in NHS financing and staff rotas that challenged patient safety and continuity of patient care. Over lunch in July 2014, our co-founders realised the urgent need for a 'one-stop' platform directly connecting Locum Doctors to the NHS.

 

IS THE WEBSITE SECURE?

Yes! iClick Med has been built with a very secure and versatile technology. We meet the vigorous legal requirements for storing your personal information. You are safe with us! 

 

HOW DOES IT WORK?

HOW DO I REGISTER?

1. Click on the ‘Register’ button and enter your basic details.

2. Check you email for the activation link and your unique username (don’t forget to check your spam box!).

3. Click on the activation link that will lead you to 'My Profile'. 

4. Complete your 12-station registration process (this process takes approximately 15 minutes). To help you

    track your registration, each station will lit green when all the mandatory information is entered and saved.

5. Once all the 12 stations have been lit green, you can immediately view your auto-generated CV .

6. Click on 'Upload Documents' to upload the listed mandatory documents.

6. Once the above steps have been completed your progress bar on your 'Dashboard' will become 100%

7. Once your progress bar is 100%, you agency will verify your registration and uploaded documents (please

    note until you are successfully verified you will not be able to apply to any jobs).

8. Whilst your agency is verifying your registration, please complete your profile by clicking on 'Job preferences' and 'My Availability' 

 

WHAT IS YOUR 12-STATION REGISTRATION PROCESS?

1.    Personal information

2.    Educational and Professional Qualifications

3.    Training courses

4.    Achievements

5.    Professional Registration

6.    Employment

7.    Referees

8.    Revalidation & Appraisal

9.    Safeguarding

10.  Medical History

11.  Payment setup details

12.  Declarations & Signatures

 

WHAT TYPES OF EMPLOYMENT OPTIONS ARE OPEN TO ME?

The most common forms of emploment for locum doctors are (required documents in brackets):

 

1.   PAYE (P45 or P60).

2.   Limited Company  (Certificate of incorporation & ank statement).

3.   Self-employed  (UTR number or if unavailable, a letter with your tax office address confirming that you are 

      registered as self-employed with the HMRC).

 

CAN I DOWNLOAD MY AUTO-GENERATED CV?

You may see your auto-generated CV as soon as you complete the 12-station registration process. You can edit it and download a copy at any time.

 

WHAT MANDATORY DOCUMENTS DO I NEED TO UPLOAD?

1.    Photo Identification (must be Passport)

2.    If applicable: VISA documents (and any supporting letters)

3.    Proof of Address x 2 (e.g. Utility Bill, Bank Statement, Driving License, Council Tax)

4.    Recent digital passport photo (e-copy)

5.    Primary Medical Qualification and relevant certified translation

6.    GMC full original certificate 

7.    Annual retention GMC certificate/ letter

8.    References x 2 (must be consultants)

9.    Appraisal (issued within last 12 months)

10.  CRB/ DBS certificate or details of your online DBS Registration

11.  Basic or Advanced Life Support certificate 

12.  Professional indemnity insurance certificate 

13.  Occupational Health certificate (including EPP serology reports)

14.  If applicable: Additional training certificates 

 

WHAT IS 'MY AVAILABILITY'?

You are in control of when you want to work. Click on 'My Availability' to select the dates and type of shifts you want to work for as long as you want to receive job invitations. Remember to update your availability as soon as any changes happen!

 

WHAT IS 'MY JOB PREFERENCES'?

1.   Choose the locality (post code and radius around it) that you are willing to travel within for work. 

2.   Select your Grade and your preferred specialties and sub-specialties. 

3.   Set the minimum hourly rate you are willing to work for.

4.   Finally, set the frequency with which you want to receive email notifications for suitable jobs.

 

Hospitals and your agency can now find you and send jobs invitations. Remember to update your job preferences as soon as your circumstance change!

 

WHAT IS 'REFER A FRIEND'?

Enjoy the benefits for referring a friend to your agency. Simply go to ‘My Area’, select ‘Refer a Friend’ and follow the instructions on screen to send your friend the link to registration.

 

WHAT IS 'MY DASHBOARD'?

We help you stay organised. On your Dashboard you will see:

 

1.  A map of available jobs near you. 

2.  Summary of your locum work and earnings.

3.  Status of your job bookings by selected calendar day. 

 

WHAT IS 'MY JOBS'?

Keep track of your jobs. ICM organises your jobs into Applications, Invitations from hospitals, Confirmed Bookings and Completed jobs. 

 

HOW DO I SEARCH FOR JOBS?

It is really simple! Go to your dashboard, click on 'Find a Job', and enter your specifications (specialty, grade, hourly rate, shift, location) into the job search tool. All available matching jobs in your location will be populated for you. Make your choice and apply in only a few clicks.

 

TELL ME ABOUT JOB INVITATIONS?

When your agency has verified your compliance, they will make you visible to hospitals on the website. Hospitals with jobs with jobs matching your criteria can find you and send you a job invitation.

 

HOW DO I GET BOOKED?

A hospital or agency may send a job invitation or respond to your application by sending you a notification of being shortlisted, and later on an offer for the job. The button next to the pending job application will read ‘Accept’. Once you click ‘Accept’ you have been booked. You will then have access to the induction pack and information on the job details. This information can be accessed in your account at any time.

 

HOW DO I SUBMIT MY TIME-SHEET?

As soon as you are booked for a job, your time sheet is auto-generated for you by ICM. 

At the end of your shift, you can submit your timesheet as follows:

 

1.  Go to pending time sheets.

2.  Click on the details icon of the selected timesheet.

3.  Make any adjustments for late starts or overtime, and hit save.

4.  Download and print your updated timesheet from the website. 

5.  Get the hard copy authenticated with an authorised signature from the hospital signatory and your own signature.

6.  Upload a scanned copy and click Submit.

7.  The Agency and Hospital now each have copies of your timesheet. 

 

HOW DO I KEEP TRACK OF MY TIMESHEETS?

The auto-generated job number for your placement also becomes your timesheet and invoice number. ICM will auto-generate unique numbers for different assignments. 

 

HOW DO I GET PAID?

As soon as your timesheet and invoice is approved you will be paid. The method payment will depend on your employment status (e.g PAYE, LTD, Umbrella etc).
