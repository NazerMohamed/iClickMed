become part of us

Being doctors ourselves, we are familiar with those pestering phone calls asking for your availability, timesheets or whatever it maybe. iClick Med offers you control of your locum work from registration to timesheets and invoices, whether you work full-time or part-time. Your registration is all online, so no more time wasting with paperwork. With iClick Med, you will manage your work preferences online for your Agency and Hospital to view, which includes your minimum rate per hour, your availability calendar, preferred shift type, location and much more!
